Who We Are

With over 28 years of construction experience, Sunnyside Builders Inc. has established itself as a highly trusted licensed framing contractor in San Jose, California. We have worked with diverse industries and completed numerous residential projects. As a contractor, we are committed to delivering services with the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and quality. Our firm specializes in high-quality residential home framing and commercial wood framing. We have successfully undertaken projects ranging from apartment complex, gas stations, and city projects to world-class hotels.
